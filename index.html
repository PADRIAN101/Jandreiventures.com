About me

[Jandreiventures (n.)] is a term use to describe my life adventures. I am a Certified Public Accountant by profession. Living and working in the British Virgin Islands. When we do things with intentions, it's called 'purpose', but in life it's called Philosophy. Here are some of my Philosophies: The body is made to move, so it motivated me to venture sports. Writing makes the mind stay at peace, so I write. Variety is the spice of life, so I travel. I am life-long learner. I want to know more about my craft, to know what more skills I am capable of and to know more what's in the world to see. This blog is my diary, an open book that will keep records of my adventures!
